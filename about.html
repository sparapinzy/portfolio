About Me

I am an artist inspired by the beauty of nature, the chaos of cities, and the quiet moments in between. My work explores the relationship between light, color, and emotion, using both traditional and digital mediums.

Through my paintings, I aim to create a space where viewers can pause, reflect, and connect with their own inner landscapes.
